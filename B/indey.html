About Stallion Steelworks

Founded in 2005, Stallion Steelworks has become Arizona's premier steel erection company specializing in equine facilities. Our team combines decades of steel construction experience with a deep understanding of horse behavior and needs.

We serve the entire state of Arizona, from Tucson to Phoenix, Flagstaff to Yuma, bringing top-quality steel structures to ranches, breeding facilities, training centers, and private horse owners.

All our structures are:

  • Engineered to withstand Arizona's extreme weather
  • Designed with horse safety and comfort as top priorities
  • Built with American-made steel and components
  • Customizable to your specific needs
