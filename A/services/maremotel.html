FAQs

Do you offer tack rooms, wash stations, and hay barns?

Yes—these are popular add‑ons. We'll right‑size each module and align the roof lines for a cohesive look.

What finishes are available?

Common choices include powder‑coat in dark neutrals, galvanized steel for maximum durability, or sealed raw steel for an industrial aesthetic.

Do you ship or install outside Arizona?

We primarily serve Arizona but can discuss select out‑of‑state projects. Get in touch and share your location (especially our kennels).
