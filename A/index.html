About Stallion Steelworks

Founded by master fabricators with a passion for equestrian sports, Stallion Steelworks brings together decades of metalworking expertise with an intimate understanding of high-end equine facilities.

Our Philosophy

We believe the structures housing your valuable equine partners should be as strong, elegant, and purposeful as the animals themselves. Every weld, every joint, every finish is executed with this principle in mind.

Why Steel?

Steel offers unparalleled strength-to-weight ratio, longevity, and design flexibility. Unlike wood, our steel structures won't rot, warp, or provide habitat for pests. They withstand extreme weather and maintain their structural integrity for decades.

Our Process

  1. Consultation: We listen to understand your needs, preferences, and property characteristics.
  2. Design: Our team creates custom plans balancing functionality and aesthetics.
  3. Fabrication: Components are precision-fabricated in our climate-controlled workshop.
  4. Installation: Our expert crews install with minimal disruption to your property.
  5. Follow-up: We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties.
